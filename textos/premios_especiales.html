SPECIAL AWARDS

  • COSTUME AWARD
    Recognition for the choreography with the most creative costumes that complement the vision of the routine.
  • ELEVATED TECHNIQUE AWARD
    This recognition will be awarded to the choreography and dancers demonstrating an advanced level of dance technique
  • POWER AWARD
    This recognition will be awarded to the choreography with the most energy that excites the audience and judges
  • POWER SASS
    This recognition will be awarded to the choreography with the most attitude and stage presence.
  • CREATIVE AWARD
    Recognition for the most creative, original, and imaginative choreographies, as determined by the judges.
